40 Luxury Safari Tents & 10 Ultra VIP Tents in the Jungle

40 Luxury Tents & 10 Ultra VIP Tents

A Home Within the Jungle

Each of our 50 private tents is a sanctuary unto itself — set deep within the jungle corridor, elevated above the forest floor on three-foot dark mahogany walkways, and appointed with the finest materials and furnishings. Choose from 40 Luxury Safari Tents or one of just 10 exclusive Ultra VIP Tents — larger, richer in technology, and elevated in every dimension of service.

Luxury Tent — Exterior, Daytime
The Architecture

The Curved Canvas Exterior

The iconic silhouette of the Isla Aguada tent is defined by its gracefully curved canvas roof — a form that evokes the grand expedition camps of a century past while providing exceptional structural integrity and climate comfort.

Viewed from without, each tent emerges organically from the jungle, its natural tones blending seamlessly with the surrounding canopy. The structure sits proud on its elevated mahogany platform, connected to the resort's winding walkway network.

  • Curved expedition-grade canvas roof construction
  • 3-foot elevated dark mahogany platform and walkways
  • Hidden within jungle canopy for complete privacy
  • Natural colour palette harmonious with the environment
  • Structural design for tropical climate conditions
Luxury Tent — Exterior, Sunset
The Atmosphere

Golden Hour & Edison Glow

As the sun descends towards the Gulf horizon, the tents of Isla Aguada transform into glowing lanterns within the darkening jungle. Edison bulb lighting — warm, amber, and perfectly calibrated — suffuses the canvas walls with a magical luminescence.

This is the hour when the resort comes alive most fully: the scent of the jungle intensifies, the sounds of the canopy shift, and the glow of your private tent becomes the most inviting place on earth.

  • Edison bulb interior lighting throughout
  • Canvas walls that glow warmly at dusk
  • Accordion glass doors open to the jungle
  • Private verandah for sunset viewing
  • Complete seclusion from neighbouring tents
The Interior

Where Marble Meets Canvas

Step through the accordion glass doors and enter a world of refined luxury. Carrara marble floors cool beneath bare feet. A king-sized bed dressed in premium linens occupies centre stage. The carefully curated interior balances raw, natural materials with the precise comforts of a five-star suite.

Interior Specification

Every Detail, Considered

The interior of each tent represents the fullest expression of the Isla Aguada philosophy: that luxury in the wild is not a contradiction but an aspiration. Every material, every fitting, every piece of furniture has been selected to create an environment that is simultaneously elemental and exquisite.

  • Italian Carrara marble floor throughout
  • King-sized bed with premium linen dressing
  • Full-width accordion glass doors to the jungle
  • Edison bulb lighting — warm and dimmable
  • Dark mahogany furniture and finishing
  • Expedition-aesthetic décor and artwork
  • En-suite private shower pavilion
  • Climate control for year-round comfort
  • Curated minibar with local spirits and provisions

The Pinnacle of the Resort

Ultra VIP Tents

Ten exclusive Ultra VIP tents sit at the deepest, most secluded reaches of the jungle corridor — larger in footprint, richer in technology, and elevated in every dimension of service. For those who demand the very finest, these are in a category entirely their own.

📐

Larger Footprint

Significantly larger than the standard luxury tents, the Ultra VIP tents offer expanded living areas, a dedicated lounge space, a private outdoor terrace, and generous indoor-outdoor flow — more space to breathe, relax, and make the jungle truly your home.

📱

Premium Technology

Smart climate control, integrated sound systems with curated acoustic programming, premium entertainment, high-speed private Wi-Fi, motorised accordion doors, and a dedicated digital concierge tablet — all seamlessly embedded within the expedition aesthetic.

🛁

Expanded Spa Pavilion

A larger, fully appointed spa pavilion with a soaking tub, dual rain showers, aromatherapy station, and dedicated treatment deck — designed specifically for in-suite massage, body treatments, and couples' wellness experiences.

🎩

Dedicated Private Butler

Each Ultra VIP tent is assigned its own dedicated private butler — available around the clock, anticipating every need before it is expressed. From pre-arrival personalisation to curated in-tent dining, your butler is the invisible hand behind a flawless stay.

🍽️

In-Tent Private Dining

A fully personalised private dining experience delivered to your tent — a private chef prepares a bespoke multi-course menu on your terrace, with wine pairing, candle service, and a table set worthy of the finest restaurant in the world.

⭐

Priority Everything

Priority access to all resort facilities, first booking rights on all tours and excursions, exclusive beach cabana reservation, priority tee times at Campeche Golf Club, and a bespoke pre-arrival welcome package curated to your personal preferences.
