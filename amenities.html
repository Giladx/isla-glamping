Full Resort Services

Beyond the Tent

Isla Aguada Glamping Co. is a fully-appointed resort in every sense. From the magnificent Grand Reception Pavilion to private guest services huts and individual shower pavilions, every amenity has been designed to the standard of a world-class luxury property — in the heart of the jungle.

Guest Services Hut

Each cluster of tents is served by a dedicated guest services hut — a beautifully crafted thatched structure housing your personal concierge, a curated selection of provisions, and every resource needed to enhance your stay.

Staffed around the clock, the guest services hut is your point of contact for excursion bookings, dining reservations, room service, and any special requests — all handled with the quiet efficiency of a world-class hospitality team.

Private Shower Pavilion & Outdoor Spa

Each tent at Isla Aguada is served by its own private shower pavilion — an open-air sanctuary that goes far beyond bathing. Surrounded by tropical plantings and washed in natural light, this is your personal spa retreat, available day and night.

Our dedicated spa team comes to you — no lobby, no waiting room, no crowds. Simply step onto your private pavilion deck and let the jungle do the rest.

Your Private Sanctuary

The Outdoor Spa Experience

Beyond the Resort

Expeditions & Activities

Isla Aguada sits at the gateway to some of Mexico's most extraordinary natural and cultural destinations — all within easy reach via our dedicated shuttle service.

⛳

Campeche Golf Club

The Campeche Golf Club is just 30 minutes away — and our dedicated shuttle service will take you there and back in complete comfort. Spend a full day on the fairways, with the Gulf of Mexico as your backdrop, then return to your jungle tent for a sunset cocktail.

Shuttle service included. Tee times arranged by concierge.

🏛️

Calakmul — Ancient Maya City

Journey deep into the Yucatan jungle to Calakmul — one of the greatest and most remote Maya cities ever discovered, now a UNESCO World Heritage Site and Biosphere Reserve. Our expert guides lead full-day expeditions into this extraordinary lost world, rarely visited and profoundly moving.

Full-day guided tours. Expert naturalist and archaeological guides.

🏰

Old City Campeche — The Walled City

Just 30 minutes from Isla Aguada, the UNESCO World Heritage walled city of Campeche is one of the most captivating and best-preserved colonial cities in all of the Americas. Stroll cobblestone streets lined with buildings painted in vivid Caribbean hues — turquoise, coral, saffron, and ochre — framed by 17th-century Spanish fortifications built to repel Caribbean pirates. Explore the bustling central market, artisan workshops, cathedral squares, and a thriving local food scene rooted in centuries of Mayan and Spanish tradition. An afternoon here is a journey through 500 years of living history, colour, and culture.

30 minutes by road · Shuttle service available · UNESCO World Heritage City

🚣

Laguna Tours

Glide through the mirror-still waters of the Laguna de Términos — one of Mexico's largest and most biodiverse coastal lagoons. Kayak or take a guided boat tour through mangrove channels teeming with birds, manatees, crocodiles, and endemic wildlife found nowhere else on earth.

Morning and sunset tours available. All equipment provided.

🪂

Wind Sports on the Beach

The Gulf Coast winds make our beach perfect for kitesurfing, windsurfing, and stand-up paddleboarding. We offer wind sports only — a deliberate and proud policy. No motor boats, no jet skis, no noise. The wildlife of the Gulf — dolphins, sea turtles, and coastal birds — thrive here precisely because we protect them.

Kitesurfing · Windsurfing · SUP · No motor sports. Ever.

Maya Cultural Expeditions
🏘️
Living Culture Campeche Interior

Living Maya
Village Tours

Step beyond the resort and into the living world of the Maya — communities that have called this peninsula home for over two thousand years. Our expert cultural guides lead intimate small-group visits to authentic Maya villages in the Campeche interior, where daily life, ancient customs, traditional food, artisanal crafts, and the deep relationship between people and land are still very much alive.

You will visit family milpas — the sacred corn fields at the heart of Maya cosmology — witness traditional cooking techniques passed down across generations, and be welcomed into a way of life the wider world has barely touched. These are not performances. They are real communities, real people, and real stories.

Small Groups Only Cultural Guide Half or Full-Day

Ancient Kingdoms UNESCO Heritage Sites

Maya Archaeological
Site Tours

The Campeche region is one of the most archaeology-rich corridors in the entire Maya world — and Isla Aguada sits at its doorstep. Beyond the world-famous ruins of Calakmul, our guides lead expeditions to lesser-known but extraordinary sites: Edzná, with its rare five-storey pyramid; Balamkú, sheltering a breathtaking stucco frieze largely unseen by the outside world; and sites still being actively excavated by INAH archaeologists.

Walk temple staircases. Stand in plazas where kings once held court. Read the carved stelae that record dynasties, battles, and celestial events. With an expert archaeologist at your side, these stones stop being ruins — and start being stories.

Isla Aguada — Pueblo Mágico

Isla Aguada holds the prestigious Pueblo Mágico designation — Mexico's highest honour for towns of exceptional cultural, historical, and natural significance. Awarded by the Mexican government to communities that offer a unique and magical experience, this title places Isla Aguada among an elite group of Mexico's most extraordinary destinations.
