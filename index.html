The Experience

Inspired by the golden age of safari and the romance of El Dorado expeditions, every detail of Isla Aguada is designed to transport you to a world of discovery and wonder.

🌿

Jungle Immersion

40 luxury safari tents and 10 exclusive Ultra VIP tents hidden within a pristine jungle corridor, each elevated on dark mahogany walkways.

🌊

Gulf Frontage

Direct access to the turquoise waters of the Gulf of Mexico along our private beach zone.

🏛️

Grand Reception

A magnificent thatched pavilion housing a full resort bar, concierge, and 100+ capacity social lounge.

🍹

Mas'Tul Bar & Restaurant

A legendary circular two-storey thatched pavilion — fire shows, live music, belly dancers, world-class cocktails and panoramic Gulf views until 2 AM.

⛺

Safari Tents

Curved canvas roofs, Carrara marble floors, accordion glass doors, Edison lighting and king-sized beds in every tent.

🦋

Campeche, Mexico

Set on a UNESCO-recognised coastline rich with endemic wildlife, mangroves, and the legends of the ancient Maya.
