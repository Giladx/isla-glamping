Safety & Security

Why Isla Aguada is Mexico's Safest & Most Protected Destination

When choosing a destination, safety is everything. At Isla Aguada, Campeche, we can offer something that almost no other resort in Mexico — or the world — can match: a complete, verifiable guarantee of safety across every dimension that matters to the modern traveller.

Three Pillars of Safety

Unmatched on Every Level

🌀

Zero Hurricanes

In over 50 years of recorded hurricane history, not a single hurricane has made landfall at Isla Aguada. While the Caribbean and Riviera Maya are battered season after season, Campeche's Gulf Coast remains untouched.

🛡️

Lowest Crime Rate in Mexico

Campeche consistently ranks as the safest state in all of Mexico — year after year. With the lowest crime rate in the country, guests walk freely, explore confidently, and sleep soundly. No travel advisories. No concerns.

💚

First Green in Covid

Campeche was the first state in Mexico to achieve Green status during the Covid-19 pandemic — a testament to its exceptional public health governance, low population density, and the natural protection afforded by its geography.

Personal Safety

The Safest State in Mexico

Campeche is not merely safe by Mexican standards — it is safe by the standards of any country in the world. Year after year, independent surveys and government statistics confirm what visitors already know: this is a place where you can truly relax.

🏆 Ranked #1 Safest State

Campeche holds the distinction of being ranked the number one safest state in Mexico by national crime statistics. With an extraordinarily low incidence of both violent and petty crime, it represents a level of personal security that rivals the safest destinations in Europe and North America.

🚶 Walk Freely, Explore Freely

Unlike many resort destinations in Mexico where guests are advised to stay within resort boundaries, Isla Aguada and the surrounding Campeche region invite you to walk freely at any hour, explore local villages, visit markets, and engage with the warmth of the local community without concern.

📋 No Travel Advisories

Campeche is one of the very few states in Mexico that carries no travel warnings or advisories from any major Western government — including the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, and Australia. Travel with complete confidence and peace of mind.

🤝 A Welcoming Community

Isla Aguada's Pueblo Mágico designation reflects not just the beauty of the place, but the character of its people. The community is warm, proud, and deeply connected to their land and traditions — making every interaction with the local culture a highlight of your stay.

Public Health

First Green in Mexico — Covid-19

During the global Covid-19 pandemic, Campeche made international headlines by becoming the first state in Mexico to achieve Green status — the highest level of health safety under Mexico's national traffic-light system — and one of the first regions in all of Latin America to do so.

This achievement was the result of Campeche's exceptional public health infrastructure, the discipline and cooperation of its communities, and the natural advantages of a low-density coastal environment. It demonstrated, once again, that Campeche operates to a standard all its own.

For travellers who value health, cleanliness, and governance alongside beauty and luxury, Campeche's record speaks for itself.

The Beach Guarantee

No Sargassum. Guaranteed.

The Gulf Coast of Campeche does not receive Sargassum. This is not a seasonal claim or a hopeful promise — it is a geographical fact. The Atlantic Sargassum bloom that devastates Caribbean and Riviera Maya beaches every summer is carried by ocean currents that do not reach the Campeche coastline.

Book your stay at Isla Aguada knowing that your beach will be pristine, clear, and beautiful on every single day — whether you arrive in January or August. No foul odour, no brown water, no disappointment. Unlike the Riviera Maya, Cancún, or any Caribbean destination, we make this guarantee without hesitation.

Campeche is Mexico's best-kept secret — and Isla Aguada its crown jewel. Safe, pristine, untouched by hurricanes, free of Sargassum, and alive with the magic of a Pueblo Mágico. This is where the truly discerning traveller comes.

Guest Health & Emergency Care

Medical Support — Fully Covered

Isla Aguada is not remote from medical care — it is exceptionally well served. The resort has its own on-call medical team, and guests have access to a full spectrum of clinics, hospitals, and specialist facilities across Campeche state, with a major regional hospital centre less than 30 minutes away.

🏥
On-Property

Resort Medical Team — On Call 24/7

A qualified physician and paramedic team is on call at all times within the resort. All clinical wellness treatments are administered by licensed COFEPRIS-registered doctors. A fully equipped first-response medical station is maintained on-site with defibrillator, oxygen, trauma kit, and emergency medications. Every member of resort staff is trained in basic first aid and emergency response.

On-Call Physician  ·  Paramedic Response  ·  24/7 Availability  ·  Emergency Station On-Site

The resort's concierge maintains a direct emergency line to this facility.

Adjacent to the Resort · On the Waterfront

Mexican Navy — SEMAR
Station Located Next Door

Directly adjacent to Isla Aguada Glamping Co. sits an active Secretaría de Marina (SEMAR) station — the Mexican Navy's maritime authority and the body responsible for all Search and Rescue operations in Mexican coastal waters. This is not a distant resource. This is your neighbour.

⚓ SEMAR · Secretaría de Marina

Mexico's Naval Maritime Authority — Right Next Door

The Mexican Navy (SEMAR) maintains a permanent active station immediately adjacent to the Isla Aguada resort property. SEMAR is Mexico's highest maritime authority — responsible for coastal defence, law enforcement at sea, and all maritime Search and Rescue (Búsqueda y Rescate Marítimo) operations across Mexico's Gulf and Pacific coastlines.

Their physical presence next to our property means that naval vessels, trained rescue personnel, and emergency response capability are available at the waterfront — within metres of our beach — at all times.

🚢 Maritime Search & Rescue

Búsqueda y Rescate Marítimo — Immediate Response

SEMAR's Search and Rescue operations cover the entire Gulf of Mexico coastline. With their station adjacent to the resort, response times for any maritime emergency — whether on the beach, in the Gulf waters, or on Laguna de Términos — are measured in minutes, not hours.

This is an extraordinary and rare advantage. Very few resorts anywhere in the world can place a naval maritime rescue station within direct sight of their beach. At Isla Aguada, this is simply a geographical fact.
