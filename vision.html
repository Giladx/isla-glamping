The Vision

A World-Class Destination on the Gulf of Mexico

The Concept

An Expedition Resort Unlike Any Other

Isla Aguada Glamping Co. is conceived as the Gulf Coast of Mexico's premier luxury glamping destination — a resort where the primal beauty of the Yucatan jungle meets the quiet opulence of a world-class hospitality experience.

Drawing inspiration from the golden age of expedition travel, the lost-city romances of El Dorado, and the grand safari camps of a century past, every structure and experience at Isla Aguada is designed to evoke wonder, discovery, and the particular thrill of being somewhere truly extraordinary.

The resort occupies a rare dual-zone site on Mexico's Gulf Coast — wild, pristine beaches stretching as far as the eye can see, and a sweeping jungle corridor — offering guests a complete coastal and jungle immersion.

The El Dorado / Lost City Aesthetic

The design language of Isla Aguada is rooted in the romanticism of early twentieth-century expedition culture — the thrill of discovery, the elegance of the explorer's camp, and the mystique of ancient civilisations hidden within impenetrable jungle. Thatched pavilions rise from the canopy. Dark mahogany walkways wind through the undergrowth. Edison lamps cast a warm amber glow as the Gulf breeze moves through the canvas walls of your private tent. This is adventure, civilised to perfection.

Campeche, Mexico

The Location

The state of Campeche occupies the south-western portion of the Yucatan Peninsula, fringing the Gulf of Mexico with hundreds of kilometres of pristine coastline. Here, the landscape transitions dramatically from open beach to dense tropical jungle within metres — a natural phenomenon that forms the very foundation of the Isla Aguada concept.

The site sits at coordinates 18°56′16.5″N, 91°18′24.7″W, accessed via Federal Highway 180, which elegantly divides the north beach zone from the south glamping jungle zone — creating a natural separation between sea-facing and jungle-facing experiences.

The surrounding region is home to extraordinary endemic wildlife, ancient Maya archaeological sites, the UNESCO-protected Calakmul Biosphere Reserve, and one of Mexico's finest undiscovered coastlines.

A Guaranteed Clear Beach

Unlike the Riviera Maya and the Caribbean, the Gulf Coast of Campeche never receives Sargassum. Guests at Isla Aguada are guaranteed pristine, crystal-clear shores on every single day of their stay — no seaweed, no odour, no disappointment. Your beach holiday is truly guaranteed.

The Philosophy

Luxury Without Compromise

At Isla Aguada, we believe that being in nature should never mean sacrificing comfort. Our tents are designed to the standard of the finest boutique hotels — yet they breathe with the life of the jungle around them.

Materials

Carrara marble floors. Dark mahogany timber. Thatched indigenous ceilings. Canvas walls of expedition-grade quality. Every material selected for beauty, durability, and its harmony with the natural surroundings.

Atmosphere

Edison bulb lighting. The amber warmth of lanterns at dusk. The cool air of the jungle moving through accordion glass doors. A king bed dressed in the finest linens. The sound of birdsong at dawn. This is the Isla Aguada atmosphere.

Service

A full concierge team. Expert naturalist guides. A bar stocked with the finest spirits. Chef-curated menus celebrating the flavours of Campeche and the Yucatan. Every need anticipated before it is felt.

Discovery

Kayaking through mangrove channels. Sunrise yoga on the beach. Expeditions to ancient Maya sites. Night-time bioluminescence swims. Cooking classes with local masters. Every day a new discovery awaits.
