Nutrition-Focused Activities

Knowledge is the most powerful supplement. Alongside your personal nutrition programme, we offer immersive educational experiences that equip you with the understanding and skills to continue your transformation at home — for life.

Cooking Demonstrations & Classes

Hands-on sessions in our open-air jungle kitchen with our Executive Chef and Nutritionist working side by side. Learn to prepare organic, anti-inflammatory, and deeply nourishing meals using ingredients harvested from our garden that morning — from Mayan medicinal herbs to fresh Gulf seafood.

Nutritional Workshops

Intimate group workshops covering the science and practice of optimal nutrition. Topics include mindful eating, the gut-brain connection, reducing systemic inflammation through diet, the microbiome and immunity, reading your blood work, and understanding the relationship between food and longevity.

The Pantry Edit

A private one-on-one session with your dietitian where you learn exactly which foods to remove and which to add to your home kitchen. You leave with a personalised shopping guide, a list of foods that are actively harming you, and a practical framework for rebuilding your pantry around functional, healing foods.

Cold-Press Juice & Smoothie Bar

A living, daily ritual — our sunrise juice and smoothie bar is staffed by our nutritionist each morning. Every blend is calibrated to your current programme phase: detox, immune boost, anti-inflammatory, energy, or hormone support. Learn to replicate each formula at home.

Farm & Garden Tours

Walk the organic gardens and production areas of Isla Aguada with our Head Gardener and Nutritionist. Understand which plants and herbs hold medicinal value, how our food is grown without pesticides or chemicals, and how the ancient Mayan relationship with plants can be integrated into your daily life.

Mindful Eating & Ritual

Guided sessions on the practice of mindful eating — slowing down, tuning into hunger and satiation signals, understanding emotional eating patterns, and building a healthier relationship with food. Conducted in the jungle at sunrise or sunset, these sessions are as meditative as they are transformative.
